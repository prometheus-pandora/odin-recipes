Chicken Noodle Soup

A Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup Bowl.

Soup that is good for the body and soul!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup (60mL) of Olive oil
  • 1 Large onion, roughly diced
  • 2 Large carrots, sliced into rings
  • 3 celery stalks, sliced
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 3 Garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  • 8 cups (2L) of Chicken stock
  • 8 oz (225g) of Egg noodles
  • 1.5lb (500g) of Chicken breast, roughly diced
  • 1/2 cup (10g) of Fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup (5g) of Fresh dill, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil until shimmering over medium heat in a large soup pot.
  2. Add the onion, carrots, celery, and 1 teaspoon each of salt and pepper. Saute, stirring frequently, until the vegetables are very soft, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant and lightly golden, about 1 minute. Deglaze with a splash of stock then add the remaining stock, bringing it to a gentle boil.
  4. Add the noodles and cook until al dente, reduce to a simmer, then add the diced chicken and cook until completely white in the center.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper, then stir in the parsley (and dill if using).
  6. Garnish with celery leaves (optional).
