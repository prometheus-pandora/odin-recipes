Scrambled Eggs

Description

Make a delicious breakfast! Or lunch. Or dinner.

Makes a meal for 2.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon (14g) of butter
  • Salt

Instructions

  • Start heating a nonstick pan to medium heat.
  • Crack the eggs into a bowl, and whisk the eggs vigorously with a pinch of salt to combine well.
  • Foam 1 tablespoon (14g) of butter in the pan, then add the beaten eggs.
  • Constantly push the eggs around the pan with a flat-edged spatula.
  • Remove from heat while still a little wet.
  • Give a final stir and serve on toast (or avocado toast!)
